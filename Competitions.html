How the Season runs

Our Spring Season runs from September through to December, wrapping up before school breaks up for the year — the exact finish date depends on the weather. Games are played through the September/October school holidays.

Every match is two 18-minute halves, with a short break at half-time and a gap between matches to keep the day running on schedule. An official timekeeper controls the clock, and every game is refereed — the referee's decision is final.

Each registered player gets a full uniform to keep — jersey, shorts and socks. Every team's jersey is a different design, so there's no need for bibs or alternate strips — players are required to wear their own team's jersey whenever they play.

Competitions are generally run with six, eight or ten teams, graded as evenly as possible based on the details provided at registration. Every team needs a coach or manager on the sideline to run substitutions and act as the main point of contact — an email address is required for team correspondence.

Age Groups & Team Rules

A player's age group is determined by how old they are as of December 31st in the competition year.

Player and team restrictions vary slightly by competition, aimed at keeping things fair and competitive for everyone:

  • Mixed Under 6–9: teams can be made up of any combination of boys and girls
  • Girls' competitions (Under 6/7 through Under 17): girls-only, with a maximum of two representative-level players per team
  • Boys' competitions (Under 10–17): girls are welcome to play but not as goalkeeper, with a maximum of two representative-level players per team
  • Under 20s (Boys & Girls): maximum of two representative, PL or AL1-level players per team
  • Mixed Open Age: each team needs at least three players of each gender, with at least two female players on the field at all times; female players don't play goalkeeper; a maximum of two representative players per team; this is a social competition and rough play isn't tolerated
  • Men's & Ladies' Open Age and Over 35s: maximum of two representative, PL or AL1-level players per team
