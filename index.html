What do you get when you play Football SSG?

FootballSSG is the Northern Beaches' longest-running, best organised and most fun 6-a-side football competition.

Included in your registration fee is, well, pretty much everything. We provide every player with a full playing kit (jersey, shorts and socks) plus a goalkeeper jersey for each team. We provide referees for every game, plenty of match footballs on every field for every game, official time keepers, on site assistance and the best, clear, accurate, relevant and timely communications that you'll experience in a community-based sporting organisation.

You simply need to organise your team of 8-10 players and a manager. We look after everything else.

The role of the manager at FootballSSG is very, very simple. Your tasks are:

  • At the advised time before the season starts, collect (or have someone else collect) your team's player kits
  • Distribute your team's player kits to the players
  • Before the start of each game, complete the team's details on the Match Record Sheet
  • During the game, organise (or have someone else organise) the player substitutions such that every player gets a fair go
  • At the conclusion of each game, sign the Match Record Sheet to confirm your agreement with the score as recorded

When we established the competition back in 2007, we wanted to create a place where players could just come and have fun, where parents could enjoy themselves and not feel pressured to 'volunteer' and where the referees and field officials felt safe and supported as they learn to be the best they can be. All these years later, with various 'tweaks' along the way, we're confident we've got things just about as right as they can be.
